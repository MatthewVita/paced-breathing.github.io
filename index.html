Paced Breathing

In Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), one of the Distress Tolerance Skills is Paced Breathing. This breathing technique cues one's parasympathetic nervous system by breathing deeply and in a timed-arranged way. Dr. Marsha M. Linehan, creator of DBT, notes that this technique is a "Behavioral Anti-Anxiety Medication".

When ready, click "begin" and follow the breathing guides until you have reached a calmer state (3-4 minutes is a typical session length - a timer is provided to the bottom right).
